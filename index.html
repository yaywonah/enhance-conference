Overview

The Enhance Conference group is dedicated to helping advance the professional goals of pharmacists and students. We do this by holding professional career conferences such as the session on Residency Post- Match directions (Post-Match Huddle) and our premier conference on lifestyle-driven career planning and various careers.

The reality of our lives dictates that working and personal situations can change quickly. To decrease this variability, our work takes a very personal and “inside out” approach to developing you as a person first, professional second. Finally, we ensure that you are applying the learning to your own personalized plan that you create in real time, AND have the knowledge of how to make it stick.

Who We Are

Our speakers are from diverse backgrounds such as consulting, health-system, clinical, academic, public health, and government settings.
